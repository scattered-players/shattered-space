Shattered Space is an online, devised, three-dimensional, interactive show set in The Matra System. Audience members take the role of “Star Jockeys,” deployed to assist inhabitants of the places affected by T h e S h a t t e r, which has caused the binary suns “The Mothers” of the star system to disappear.

Satellites and other communications arrays have been wiped out, leaving places & space stations isolated. Not knowing who or what has survived, the emergency systems have deployed the Star Jockeys, an elite space force that is being sent to establish contact and hopefully give some aid.
